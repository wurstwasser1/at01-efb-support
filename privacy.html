AT01 EFB Privacy Policy

Effective: 30 June 2026

AT01 EFB is designed to work without an account and without advertising or third-party analytics. The developer does not sell personal information.

Information stored on your device

Aircraft settings, aircraft registration, preferred language, performance preferences, fuel-planning values, and disclaimer acceptance are stored locally on your device. The app does not transmit these settings to the developer.

Location

With your permission, the app obtains an approximate current location to sort nearby ATIS stations by distance. Location is processed on the device, is not stored by the app, and is not transmitted to the developer. You can deny or revoke location access in iOS Settings.

Weather services

The app retrieves aviation weather information from Deutscher Wetterdienst (DWD) and AviationWeather.gov. When these features are used, standard network information such as your IP address may be processed by those service providers under their respective privacy policies. The app does not add your location, aircraft settings, or personal profile to these requests.

Feedback and support

If you choose to send feedback, the app opens your email application with a draft addressed to the developer. The draft can include information you enter and device, operating-system, and aircraft-registration details shown to you before sending. Nothing is sent until you choose to send the email. Support correspondence is used only to respond to your request and troubleshoot the app.

Generated documents

Performance PDF documents are generated locally. They leave the app only when you explicitly save or share them using iOS sharing features.

Children

The app is not directed to children and the developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the app’s features or data practices change. The effective date above identifies the current version.

Contact

Privacy questions may be submitted through the AT01 EFB support tracker. Do not include personal or sensitive information in a public report.
